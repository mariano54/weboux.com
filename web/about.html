About weboux

What is weboux?

Weboux is a tutorial website to help you learn web development. Many people know a little bit of programming, but have no idea how to get a website online. And when they try to look for guides or books, they find guides that are too hard, or that only teach one thing.

We will attempt to cover from the very basics of HTML to the things required to put up a website on the internet. We will only assume knowledge of one programming language, and we will go very slow to help you undertand everything.

By the end of these lessons, you will know how to make a website, and you will know exactly how this website was made. From the logo, to the animations, the the HTML, to the servers. In the end you will see the source code, and how everything was put together.
